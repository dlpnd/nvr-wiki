# Bloom

---

## Main

### Treshold

treshold value above which lights will bloom

>Default: 0.0

---

### Scale

scale the brightness of the bloom

>Default: 1.8

---

### Strength

power of the bloom when applied

>Default: 2.0

---

### Blending

How much of the bloom is visible

>Default: 0.04

---

### BloomExponent

Makes the bloom more spread out or more focused

>Default: 1.0

---
