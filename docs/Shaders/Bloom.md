# Bloom

---

## Main

### Blending

Blending between the base and bloom. Low values are preferable. Works for NVR and vanilla.

>Default: 0.04

---

### Strength

Scales the bloom. Works for NVR and vanilla.

>Default: 1.0

---

### Passes

Passes to apply, 2-8.

>Default: 8

---

### PassBlending

0: Use additive blending, 0-1: Linear blending coefficient.

>Default: 0.0
