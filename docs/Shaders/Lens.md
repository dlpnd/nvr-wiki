# Lens

---

## Main

### Strength

Global effect strength multiplier.

>Default: 0.4

---

### BloomExponent

Determins how far from the light source the dirt particles are lit

>Default: 0.7

---

### Smudginess

Scales the texture so that more of the screen is affected

>Default: 0.0
